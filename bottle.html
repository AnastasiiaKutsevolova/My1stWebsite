BOTTLE

Bottle Sibolle™ – a unique recyclable capacity for drinking water that has no analogues in the world.The bottle is compatible with existing drinking water bottling systems and coolers with internal and external bottle placement and other industry-standard equipment. Our bottle is filled with Sibolle Alpine™ and it ensures the hygienic and safe use of drinking water, as well as preserving its original natural properties for a long time. This achievement has become possible through the use of high-tech Sibolle™ bottles designed and engineered in Ukraine and produced in Italy.
