The world's first eco-friendly water coolers

Sibolle Alpine ™ - alkaline alpine spring water from Austrian Styria. Water naturally enriched with oxygen has a rare, ideally balanced mineral composition, provides the body with all the necessary micro and macro elements. It originates at an elevation of 2277 meters above sea level and passes through the territory of the water protection zone, protected by the state since the time of Emperor Franz Joseph. It acquires its outstanding properties through self-filtration throughout the year through the alpine rocks of the Austrian national reserve in the Hochschwab region, which invariably guarantees its high quality. And also Sibolle Alpine ™ rightfully claims to be one of the world's best high-altitude spring waters.
