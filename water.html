Water

Sibolle Alpine™ – natural alpine spring water from the Austrian state of Styria. The water is characterized with a unique and balanced mineralization as well as a high content of natural oxygen to ensure the intake of all the necessary micro- and macro- elements. It flows from the highest summit with a height of 2,277 m above sea level and passes through the territory which is protected by the state since the time of Emperor Franz Josef. It acquires its outstanding qualities during the whole year of filtration through various rock formations of the Alps in the Hohschwab region in Austria, which consistently guarantees its high quality and with full rights to claim the title of one of the best high-mountain spring water in the world.
